Are You Ready To Get Dialled In?

Explore Richmond on foot, learn about all the amazing places to visit and how to better protect our natural world. We provide guided walks through the borough using our grade II listed phone boxes as destination points, which unlock gamified content. Walk, play and supercharge your environmental efforts.

Give your walks some purpose!

Why take part?

  • • Guided walks showing you the best of the borough
  • • Games and quizzes to play for all ages, including some GEO cache locations
  • • Information and resources which increase your environmental knowledge
  • • Special access to local artists' work which represents each phone box's theme
  • • Be part of a walking community and keep fit while making friends
  • • Earn activity badges and compete with everyone on the network.

Let's Get Connected!

Our walks will be launching in June 2026. Stay in touch on social media for more information after we have launched.

Meet the Operator

Box Walks is a registered charity on a mission to get the United Kingdom exploring and taking part in environmental action, while bringing our iconic red grade II listed phone boxes out of retirement and giving them a new purpose. We are launching our first walks in Richmond Upon Thames, with plans to expand the network as more people take part in our walks and complete environmental actions.
