Meet the Operator

Box Walks is a registered charity which develops sustainability-themed, adventure walks using Britain's grade II listed phone boxes as destination points. The phone boxes unlock gamified content and activities.

Our vision is to bring our urban grade II listed phone boxes out of retirement, and once again use them to create connection and community. Only this time, amplifying their reach to reflect our modern world.

What we do

We protect and maintain Britain's grade II listed phone boxes, preserving our architectural and cultural heritage, while ensuring future generations can enjoy them.

We accelerate environmental action by providing our communities with activities they can complete to reduce their environmental footprint, while collecting points and competing with others.

How we do it

We buy and lease the phone boxes, and make sure these are maintained and kept in good condition. We preserve the integrity of all our phone boxes by holding the content relating to our walks and activities on our Box Walks app.

Everyone can download the Box Walks app for free, set up an account, take part in walks and activities, earn points and compete with others on the app. They will, however, need to visit each physical phone box to be able to unlock different walks and activities held on the app.

Our trustees

Emma Howcroft

Emma Howcroft is the chair and co-founder of Box Walks. She is a transformational sustainability and procurement leader and comes with 22 years' experience working in multinational companies. Her recent projects include the design and implementation of a sustainable sourcing programme for a life sciences company and a sustainable plastics programme for a FTSE 100 pharma company. Emma has a Master's in Sustainability Leadership from the University of Cambridge and sits on various nature-related advocacy groups which help organisations embed natural capital consideration into their business processes.

At Box Walks, Emma is leading the effort to transition the charity from proof of concept to operational launch, establishing the governance, funding, and compliance structures needed to scale up its impact. She also works with the other trustees to create the content for the walks and activities.
